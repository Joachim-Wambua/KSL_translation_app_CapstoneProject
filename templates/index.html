About

This web application was created with the intention of revolutionising sign language communication for the fourth industrial revolution. This application uses a machine(deep) learning model to analyse video sequences and convert them to their corresponding word meanings.

This application is designed for persons with hearing & speech impairments and their guardians/partners as it aims to assist them in day to day communication & interactions.
